### CoachLink Baseball — Pitch Card

| | |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | SEA baseball coaching stuck in the past — geographical bottleneck, tiny local coach pool, players rely on unguided YouTube |
| **Insight** | Society normalizes online-first commerce/trust everywhere — but amateur sports coaching remains hyper-local, driven by luck on Discord or word-of-mouth |
| **Solution** | Digital marketplace connecting SEA players with world-class coaches in Japan/Taiwan via video-analysis matching |
| **MVP** | (1) Player inputs → (2) Rule-based matching → (3) Side-by-side coach comparison → (4) Single-click video upload → connects to coach |
| **Key line** | *"Great startups apply already accepted societal behaviors to highly underserved domains"* |
| **Long-term** | Hybrid ecosystem: digital trust → physical clinics, regional camps, athletic mentorship |
